Dr.Muhammad Sajid

Staff Geophysicst with 17 Years of O&G experience,
He has successfully developed several advanced seismic
technologies with practical applications in the upstream field

These technologies include algorithms for dip-guided seismic band- width expansion (non-stationary differential resolution or NSDR and structural-oriented dip-guided enhanced non-stationary differential resolution or eNSDR), e-coherency for structure- oriented denoising, spectral analysis, and decomposition, AI seismic attributes, as well as advanced Artificial Intelligence- based Seismic Features Recognition and Extraction technologies with vectorization capabilities for 2D, 2.5D, and 3D archi- tectures, seismic inversion, and reservoir characterization. Dr. Sajid has also played a pivotal role in staff development within geoscience seismic interpretation, conducting research and driving technological innovation. His work is well recognized, with publications in top journals and conferences. With over 17 years of experience in the oil industry, he currently serves as a principal geophysicist at PETRONAS, where he remains committed to exploring advanced technologies such as AI, ML, signal processing, and high-resolution reservoir characterization
